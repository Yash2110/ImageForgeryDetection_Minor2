Image Forgery

In a digital age where visuals dominate our online landscape, the authenticity of images becomes paramount. Image forgery, the art of manipulating or altering images, has become a prevalent concern. This aims to shed light on the nuances of image forgery, explore various types of deceit, and unveil the cutting-edge techniques designed to expose the truth. Understanding Image Forgery: Image forgery encompasses a spectrum of manipulations, ranging from simple alterations to sophisticated fabrications. Common types include: Copy-Move Forgery: Sections of an image are duplicated and pasted elsewhere to deceive the viewer. Splicing: Combining elements from different images to create a composite, often with the intention to mislead. Retouching: Delicate alterations made to enhance or diminish certain features, blurring the line between reality and fabrication.
